Availability
===============================================================================


Availability Management
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

* Availability is the ability of an IT serveice or other configuration item to perform its agreed function when required.

Incident
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

According to ITIL 4:

* An unplanned interruption to a service, or a reduction in the quality of a service
* An incident is the representation of an outage

Problem
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------

According to ITIL 4:

* A problem is a cause, or potential cause, of one or more incidentes
* A problem is the representation of a cause or potential cause of one or more outages
